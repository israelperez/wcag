Intorduction

These brand guidelines are a tool to ensure consistency is maintained through all published material both printed and digital for any of the Healthdirect Australia branded applications.

The visual style guide provides guidance for use by staff and external suppliers to ensure that the integrity of the Healthdirect Australia brand is maintained, creating a strong, cohesive and easily identified visual package.

The visual style guide must be used to lead the design and production of all visual elements.

Our Mission

To be a key part of a quality Australian health system by helping consumers manage their own health through leveraging technology to enable timely access to health and related services.
